Wenjing Yang, MD, PhD

Research Assistant Professor, Division of Gastroenterology & Hepatology, Northwestern University Feinberg School of Medicine

Dr. Wenjing Yang is an intestinal immunologist with interdisciplinary training in clinical medicine and basic immunology. Her research focuses on how the intestinal immune microenvironment regulates CD4⁺ T cell functional states in inflammatory bowel disease, with particular interests in regulatory T cells, tissue repair, fibrosis, and microbiota- or nutrient-derived immune signals.
